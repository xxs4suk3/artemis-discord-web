    Last updated: July 11, 2024

    • At Artemis Bot, we value the privacy of our users and are committed to protecting it. This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect the personal information of users who interact with our website, in compliance with applicable privacy laws, including the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) of the United States, and other relevant global regulations.

    Definitions

    • - Artemis Bot: Refers to our website.
    • - Personal Information: Refers to any data that can identify a user directly or indirectly.
    • - GDPR: General Data Protection Regulation of the European Union.
    • - CCPA: California Consumer Privacy Act.

    Services Used

    • At Artemis Bot, we use the following services for the operation and improvement of our site:

    • 1. Vercel: Web infrastructure provider.
    • 2. jsDelivr CDN: Content delivery network for fast and efficient loading of resources.
    • 3. Google Fonts: Service for including fonts on the website.
    • 4. Google Analytics (Universal Analytics): Tool for collecting and analyzing website traffic data.
    • 5. Google AdSense: Advertising platform that allows us to show relevant ads to users.

    Collection of Information

    • We collect the following information from users:

    • - Browsing Data: Information about your visit to the website, such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent, and links clicked. This is collected via Google Analytics.
    • - Cookies: We use cookies to enhance user experience and for analytics and advertising purposes. Cookies can be first-party or third-party (e.g., Google AdSense).
    • - Voluntarily Provided Information: Any information users provide directly, such as name, email address, and other contact details.

    Use of Information

    • The collected information is used to:

    • - Improve user experience on the website.
    • - Analyze website traffic and understand how users interact with content.
    • - Show relevant ads via Google AdSense.
    • - Protect the security and integrity of our website.
    • - Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

    Protection of Information

    • We commit to protecting the personal information of our users using commercially reasonable security measures to prevent loss, theft, unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use, or modification.

    Disclosure of Information

    • We do not share or sell users' personal information to third parties, except in the following cases:

    • - Legal Compliance: If required by law, we may disclose personal information to comply with legal processes, governmental requests, or to protect our legal rights.
    • - Service Providers: We may share information with service providers who help us operate and improve our website, always under confidentiality agreements.

    User Rights

    • Under applicable legislation, users have the following rights regarding their personal information:

    • - Access and Rectification: Users have the right to access and correct their personal information.
    • - Restriction of Processing: Users can request the restriction of the processing of their personal information in certain circumstances.
    • - Objection: Users can object to the processing of their personal information for reasons related to their particular situation.
    • - Right Not to Be Discriminated Against: Users who exercise their privacy rights under the CCPA will not be discriminated against.

    International Data Transfers

    • If we transfer your personal information outside your country of origin, we will take necessary measures to ensure that your information is adequately protected, in compliance with applicable laws.

    Contact

    • If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, you can contact us via email at privacy@artemisbot.es. You may also file a complaint with the relevant data protection authority in your jurisdiction.

    Cookies and Similar Technology

    • For more information on how we use cookies and similar technologies, please refer to our Cookies Policy.

    Consent

    • By using our website, you accept the terms of this privacy policy and consent to the processing of your personal information as described.

    Exercise of Rights

    • To exercise your privacy rights, you can contact us via privacy@artemisbot.es or through the forms available on our website.

    Data Retention

    • We only retain collected information for as long as necessary to provide you with the requested service or to fulfill legal obligations.

    Changes to the Privacy Policy

    • We reserve the right to update or modify this privacy policy at any time. It is recommended to periodically review this policy to stay informed about how we protect and use your personal information.
